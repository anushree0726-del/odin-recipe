Cake

Description

A delicious and soft homemade cake!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix butter and sugar together
  2. Add eggs and mix well
  3. Add flour and baking powder
  4. Add milk and mix into a smooth batter
  5. Pour into a baking tin
  6. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes
