Sandwich

Description

A delicious and easy sandwich recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese slices
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Take two slices of bread
  2. Spread butter on both slices
  3. Add cheese, tomato and lettuce
  4. Close the sandwich and serve!
